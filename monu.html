Why normal deletion isn't enough

When you "delete" files or empty the Recycle Bin, the OS typically removes only references — the underlying bytes remain until overwritten. Forensics tools can recover them. SSDs require cryptographic erasure for secure removal.

Risks of incomplete deletion

  • Identity theft — lost documents (IDs, bank statements)
  • Credential leaks — saved passwords, API keys
  • Corporate non-compliance — failing audits
  • Liability on resale or disposal of drives
